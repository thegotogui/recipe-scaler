The idea here is that you have a given weight of food. By default, the app will evenly divide that weight. From there, you can "bias" the portions such that one person gets maybe 10% more than the others, or one gets 10% more, another 15% less, and the third gets the remainder.
The ratio units are genuine ratios rather than percentages (for now). Hence, the units don't need to add up to anything in particular. They can simply be amounts for portioning purposes.
 
The Variable TOtal option allows you to manually specify what percentage a particular serving should consist of.
For example, if all portions are equal, and indicative of what an average serving should be, this mode allows you to override the original total in favor of one larger serving size. It also indicates how much should be added to hit that target.
